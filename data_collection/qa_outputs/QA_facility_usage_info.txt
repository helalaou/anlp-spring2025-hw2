Q: What is the percentage of the fee imposed on nonresidents using publicly funded facilities in Pittsburgh?
A: Three (3) percent.


Q: By when must the usage fee be submitted to the city of Pittsburgh?
A: No later than the 15th of the next month from the date of usage.


Q: What are the consequences for failing to remit the usage fee to the city?
A: A fine not exceeding $25,000 or imprisonment not exceeding two months, or both.


Q: What must the managing entity or nonresident contracting party do within sixty days of finalizing a contract?
A: Register with the city of Pittsburgh.


Q: Who should be contacted for more information regarding tax compliance in Pittsburgh?
A: Bryant Dill, Tax Compliance Specialist, City of Pittsburgh Department of Finance.
